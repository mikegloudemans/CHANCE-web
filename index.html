Welcome to CHANCE!

We offer free tutoring for all Durham high school and middle school students.

Get Involved:

High school and middle school students

We welcome all Durham high school and middle school students for free SAT and subject tutoring! Meetings are every Tuesday from 7-9 in the West Duke Building on Duke University's East Campus. If you're interested in attending, fill out the application and send it to us at chance-request@duke.edu.

Duke students

We're seeking Duke students to volunteer as SAT and subject tutors. It's just two hours a session. We'd love to see you here every week, but we're happy to see you even if you can only make it once in a while.

Schedule

We hold our tutoring sessions every Tuesday from 7-9 PM. The spring 2014 semester runs from January 14 through April 22. We do NOT have tutoring on March 11 due to spring break.

Directions

Tutoring sessions are held in Room 108B of the West Duke Building on Duke's East Campus.

From Main Street, turn on to Campus Drive. (This is the main entrance to East Campus.) The West Duke Building is on your left, before the traffic circle with the statue. You may park along Campus Drive, or in the parking lot marked on the map.
